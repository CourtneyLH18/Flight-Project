About


Flight is a leading UK luggage and travel accessories retailer. Founded in Newcastle in 2014, Flight aims to provide luxurious but affordable choices for everyone. Using sustainable materials, Flight puts a whole new meaning on packing lightly. Here at Flight, we believe travel experiences are what helps us grow and that is why our products are designed to last for every trip, short or long. With a focus on creating great retail experiences in-store, Flight continues to expand across the UK with 33 stores already opened.
